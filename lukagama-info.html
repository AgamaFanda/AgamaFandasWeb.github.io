Lukagama

Lukagama is a Discord bot that was developed by AgamaTheDev, LSTV, and Hrnec

for Discord BotJam #5, a competition where programmers code bots based on a specific theme.

The theme for BotJam #5 was moderation, so we created Lukagama,

a moderation bot with features like kicking, banning, muting, and

captcha verification to prevent bot raids.

If you would like to use Lukagama on your own server, feel free to do so

but keep in mind that the bot was developed in just 7 days

and will not be receiving new features. However, if you do encounter any bugs,

please let us know, and we will do our best to fix them.
